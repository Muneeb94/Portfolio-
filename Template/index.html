A Guide To The Next Steps

This guide describes the mortgage assessment process. With Match Me Legal the process is simple and hassle-free We will work free of charge to fully assess whether you may have a claim.

  • 1

    ReturnYour Encloased Forms

    These forms are called Letter’s of Authority or LOA’s. They allow us to request your mortgage file from your Lender.

  • 2

    Your Mortgage File

    Once we receive your signed LOA we request your mortgage file from your provider. They have 30 days to respond, as per data protection regulations

  • 3

    Expert Assessment

    Once we have received your file, our dedicated team will complete an in-depth audit, utilising our specialist software.

    After we have completed your assessment, we will contact you to inform you of the outcome.

  • 4

    Specialist Law Firm

    We select a firm from our panel of solicitors. They deal with your claim on a No Win No Fee* basis.

  • 5

    Staying in Touch

    As your case progresses, we will update you via text and email. If you have any questions please call us on 0161 768 0251

You are under no obligation to proceed and are welcome to pursue this independently.

You will not pay anything for work undertaken at this point.

* There may be cancellation charges once your case has been passed to a solicitor if you cancel your claim after 4 days but before it’s completed. There is no charge if you complete your claim but it is unsuccessful.
